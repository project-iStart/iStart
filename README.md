## 💡 What is iStart?

**iStart** is a community-driven platform that bridges the gap between *idea holders* and *opportunity makers*.

Every day, brilliant startup ideas never see the light — not because they lack merit, but because their creators lack access. iStart changes that.

| Role | What they do |
|:---|:---|
| 🧠 **Idea Holders** | Post, refine, and showcase startup concepts |
| 🤝 **Collaborators** | Discover teams that match their skills and passion |
| 💼 **Investors** | Scout early-stage ideas and connect with founders |
| 🚀 **Founders** | Build complete teams and manage their startup journey |

---

## ✨ Features

<details>
<summary><b>📣 Req 1 — Community Voting & Feedback</b> &nbsp; <i>Rate, vote, and shape the next big thing</i></summary>
<br/>

- ✅ Upvote / Downvote system *(founders cannot vote on their own ideas)*
- ✅ Structured feedback using predefined categories
- ✅ Aggregated **Community Score** per idea card
- ✅ Notifications when votes hit milestones or feedback is received

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>🖊️ Req 2 — Post & Manage Ideas</b> &nbsp; <i>Your idea. Your stage.</i></summary>
<br/>

- ✅ Create and publish ideas with title, description, and problem statement
- ✅ Select idea categories and startup stage
- ✅ Upload pitch deck (PDF / PPT) — securely stored and publicly accessible
- ✅ Edit, update, or delete ideas at any time

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>🔍 Req 3 — Browse & Explore</b> &nbsp; <i>Find the idea that speaks to you</i></summary>
<br/>

- ✅ Scrollable feed of all published startup ideas
- ✅ Search and filter by category, industry, or startup stage
- ✅ Detailed idea view with full information
- ✅ Bookmark / save interesting ideas

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>🤝 Req 4 — Team Collaboration</b> &nbsp; <i>The right people, the right idea</i></summary>
<br/>

- ✅ Collaborators can send join requests with skills and motivation
- ✅ Founder dashboard to approve or reject requests
- ✅ Discussion threads scoped to each startup idea
- ✅ Shared tasks and file uploads for team members

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>💰 Req 5 — Investor Discovery</b> &nbsp; <i>Spot it. Fund it. Build it.</i></summary>
<br/>

- ✅ Browse all ideas with community scores and team composition
- ✅ Follow or express interest in specific startups
- ✅ Document request messaging between investors and founders
- ✅ **"I want to fund this"** → sends contact details to founder + displays **Funding Interest Received** badge

</details>
